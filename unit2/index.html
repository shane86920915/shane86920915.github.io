Helpful Hints

  1. Certian meats, like ribs and pot roast can be parboiled before grilling to reduce the fat content.
  2. pound meat lightly with a mallet or rolling pin, pierce with a fork, sprinkle lightly with meat tenderizer and add marinade.
  3. Refrigerate for 20 minutes and cook or grill for a quick and succulent meat.
  4. Marinating is a cinch if you use a plastic bag. The meat stays in the marinade and it's easy to turn. Cleanup is easy; just toss the bag.
  5. Its easier to thinly slice meat if it is partially frozen.
