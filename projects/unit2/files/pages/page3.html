  • Baked Steak
    1. 2 lb. round steak
    2. 1 pkg. shake-n-baked for pork
    3. 1 onion, sliced
    4. 2 c. tomato juice

      5. Cut steak into serving pieces. Roll in shake-n-bake. Place in single layer in glass baking pan. Top with onion slices. Pour tomato juice over steak. Cover tightly and bake in 350 oven for 1 1/2 hours or until tender.

  • Bean Burger Bake
    1. 1 lb. ground beef
    2. 1 onion, chopped
    3. 1 can pork and beans
    4. 1 can tomato soup
    5. 2 T. brown sugar
    6. 1 T. mustard

    Brown ground beef and onion until done, drain, Add pork and beans, tomato soup, brown sugar and mustard; mix will. Cover, turn heat to low and cook 10 minutes.

  • Honey Mustard Chicken
    1. 4 skinless, boneless chicken
    2. 1/4 c. honey
    3. 1/4 c. dijon mustard
