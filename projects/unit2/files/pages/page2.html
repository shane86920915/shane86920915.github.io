  • Cheese Soup
    1. 1/4 c. chopped onion
    2. 3 T. butter
    3. 1/4 c. flour
    4. tsp. garlic powder
    5. 1/4 tsp salt & pepper
    6. 2 c. milk
    7. 1 can chicken broth
    8. 1/2 c. shredded carrots
    9. 1/2 c. finely chopped celery
    10. 1 1/2 cup shredded chedder cheese
    11. 3/4 c. shredded mozzarella cheese
    12. croutons

      13. In saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender, Add flour, salt, pepper and garlic powder; stir until smooth. Gradually add milk; cook and stir until thickened and bubble. Bring chicken broth to a boil. Add carrots and celery; simmer for 5 minutes. Add to milk mixture and stir intil blended. Add cheeses; cook and stir until melted. (Do not boil). Serve with croutons.

  • Fire Roasted Tomato Soup
    1. 2 cans tomato soup
    2. 1 can evaporated milk
    3. 14 oz. can undrained fire roasted diced tomatoes

    Garnish: sour cream, shredded cheese, crumbled bacon, croutons, if desired

    Heat tomato soup, milk and tomatoes in saucepan stirring, until hot. Serve with sour cream, shredded cheese and crumbled bacon, if desired. Makes 6 servings.

  • Meatless Chili
    1. 1 can chili beans
    2. 1 can black beans rinsed and drained
    3. 1 can light red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
    4. 2 small can tomato sauce
    5. 2 c. water
    6. 1 pkg. chili seasoning
    7. 1 can diced tomatoes

      8. Combine all ingredients in large pan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for at least 20 minutes.
