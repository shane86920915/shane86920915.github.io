  • Bacon and Chicken Livers
    1. 12 slices bacon
    2. half pound raw chicken livers
    3. 1 can sliced water chestnuts, drained, opt.
    4. Half cup soy sauce

      5. Cut bacon into thirds and liver into 1 inch pieces. Dip liver in soy sauce. Wrap piece of bacon around each piece of liver (and a water chestnut slice) and secure with toothpick. place on greased baking pan. Bake at 350 for 20-30 minutes or until bacon is cooked but not completely crisp.

  • Bacon Wings
    1. Half c. ketchup
    2. 2 cup brown sugar
    3. 1 T. Worcestershire sauce
    4. 1 T. horseradish
    5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
    6. several drops hot sauce, to taste
    7. 1 pound bacon
    8. 3 pounds chicken wings

      9. In saucepan, heat ketchup, brown sugar. W. sauce, horseradish, garlic and hot sauce for several minutes until reduced and slightly thickened. Meanwhile, cut wings into 3 pieces, discarding the tips. Cut slices of bacon in half and wrap each piece of chicken with piece of bacon. Baste chicken with some sauce and bake 20-30 minutes. Serve with reserved sauce.

  • Chili Sauce Dip
    1. 1 pound grund beef
    2. 1 onion, chopped
    3. 1 pound velveeta, cubed
    4. 1 can tomatoes with green chilies
    5. 1 tsp. chili powder
    6. 2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
    7. tortilla chips

      8. Brown ground beef and onion, drain. Add cheese, tomatoes with green chilies, chili powder and W. sauce. Mix well and heat thoroughly, stirring constantly. Serve with tortilla chips.
